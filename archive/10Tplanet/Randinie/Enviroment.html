The Environment:

Rotopia is filled with plants. Some may look deadly but none of them are. The plants are restricted areas so that people will not destroy the beauty of Rotopia. It's a whole range of plants such as flowers, bushes, trees. Basically everything on Earth but all the plants are purple, blue, pink and teal. The reason for the colours is to match the colour of the planet. They kinda look like this:

Imagine a dry area with a few lakes and the sand and dirt is purple teal. You have bunches of bushes and little weeds and some colourful trees. In Rotopia there is Winter and Spring. During the snow time it snows everywhere. It is also colder but the plants don't get affected by this and the giraffes. In the spring time the flowers grow, it's warmer so there are more activities.

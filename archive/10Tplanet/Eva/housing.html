HOUSING!

On Planet Eslie some of the houses are trees with windows and staircases inside it is literally pretty much a normal house just inside a tree theres everything you could possibly need! Including food and water, and alot of branches to hang a swing on.

There are also houses in the shape of a rose, pretty much the same as the "treehouse" just in a small rose. The rose houses are mainly for little kids playhouses as they are quite small but some are big enough for a family to live in. If you want a really fancy house you can opt for a Castle that is big enough for any family.

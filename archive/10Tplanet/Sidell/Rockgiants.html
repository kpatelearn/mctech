Rock Giants






Rock gaints haven't been seen in centuries due to the fact that they only come out of their slumber
when they are awoken by a threat to Zaimania. Due to this fact they have grown vegetation out
of the creavses of their bodys and the land around them has adapted and grown so waterfalls are
able to run down their bodies.

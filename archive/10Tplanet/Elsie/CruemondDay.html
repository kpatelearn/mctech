Life on Xena

This is a picture of one of Xenas beautiful landscape

This is what a typical house in Xena looks like. For humans on earth, these houses would be very large, expensive and rare. Here in Xena, they are very common. Because of the extreme heat in Xena, it is illegal not to own a pool. This rule was made last year when many citizens unfortunatley died due to the sun coming very close to Xena.

This is the best school in Xena called Barrett hightschool. It is a private boarding school. 25,000 kids attend Barrett Highschool. Kids that attend Barrett highschool are extremely smart and wealthy. Cruemonds major (Edward Howard Johnson) was the head boy at Barrett Highschool when he attended.

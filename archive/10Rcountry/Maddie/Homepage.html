Welcome to Maddiegascar



Maddiegascar is a small city in Tenarvia, located in the north west of the country. The bucket list city has everything from beautiful parks to gorgeous restaurants, skydiving to luxury yacht experiences, tranquil hotels to exotic art galleries. Maddiegascar has everything you could ever want, making it the perfect place to live or visit. The pristine waters that line the city are perfect for jet skiing, boating, snorkelling and every other water activity you could want.

A five star hotel sits on the water's edge, with gorgeous views of the ocean and the city. This hotel has been running for 20 years, and has hosted royal families, world leaders, and many celebrities. With 400 rooms, pools, tennis courts, gyms, and a spa, this hotel is also home to one of the country's best restaurants with award winning chefs.

Over 7 different walks and hikes can take you round the city's stunning scenery. Ranging from 30 minute walks to 4 hour hikes. Trails lead you to picturesque views of the city and the expansive ocean. Parks are spread throughout, and bikes are highly encouraged, with e-scooters and e-bikes also available for higher.

Naviguer is a yacht store, where you can either buy yourself a luxury boat or a higher one for the day or weekend. These extravagant boats come with all the amenities and range in size, colour and price depending on what you're looking for.

Adventure is just round the corner. By the airport Maddiegascars world famous skydiving experience is one of the greatest. With world class instructors and top of the line planes and gear, Ciel Haut skydiving is truly a jump to remember. With jumps running every half hour, everyone is welcome to try whether you are a beginner or advanced.

The art gallery is home to some of the world's most rare and interesting art pieces. And Café de la Forêt Tropicale is one of the best places to relax and have maddiegascars, unique coffee blends and traditional cakes. Maddiegascar has some of the best stores you can find.

Voiles. The county's best restaurant, with equisest waterfront views. Voiles has been awarded three Michelin stars. The intricate detail in every dish is phenomenal, nothing is overlooked.

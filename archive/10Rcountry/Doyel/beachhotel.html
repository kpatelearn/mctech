Crystal Beach Hotel








Our five star hotel has luxurious views to the sparkling ocean and swaying palm trees. With ten floors and located directly at the beach entrance, the Crystal Beach Hotel has a mere five minute walk to all recommended restaurants and includes rooms that are top tier.

Inside, enjoy our heated infinity pool and luxury spa, free 5G wifi, digital key card, free access to a 24 hour gym, and many more splendid amenities within the hotel. To much public approval, The Crystal Beach Hotel also allows pets of all kind to stay in the hotel so your precious animal can bathe in luxury alongside you!







Rooms available

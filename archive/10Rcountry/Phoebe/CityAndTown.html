City And Town


Welcome to the City And Towns page. Here you will briefly learn about our main town and city here in Capyparlasen! We hope you enjoy the quick enlightment of these places and take a liking to the short history behind them.


Here in Capyparlasen we have a main city and town, Sharkslen and Raintumble. Sharkslen got its name from one of the Sea Gods that first came to Capyparlasen and found their home within our lands. Sharkslen is popular for its wide range of shops and entertainment. Then we have our main town, Raintumble, quite similarly to Sharkslen, Raintumble got its name from the God that guarded the skies and protected our lands from negative weather. Raintumble is most popular for its small businesses and scenery. We hope you find enjoyment in our city and towns!

Food And Drinks



Neoveren


Neoveren is the most popular restaurant in Capyparlasen and has even been given a certificate of completion! This restaurant has the famous local drinks and food, including the Doyalia latte and Angelina crust!


Pink Sundae


Pink Sundae, a fun pink restaurant that people of all ages can agree that the enviroment is odd but welcoming. It is a restaurant that many enjoy because of the wide range of food and drinks that they offer and the friendly staff that are always wearing a smile.


Mistra


A corner cafe with the vibe of a cottage. This cafe will leave you wanting more of it's enviroment and refreshing, odd and pleasing food. Most popular for it's dandelion breakfast.


CornerCups


Corner Cups is by far the most creative food and drink store in Raintumble. Instead of using plates or bowls to server their products, they use cups that can include the customers chosen toppings. They have a varriety of desserts, such as churros, donuts and even cake!

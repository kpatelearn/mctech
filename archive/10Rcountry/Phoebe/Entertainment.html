Entertainment



Cupids Carnival


Known for its fun rides and romantic atmosphere, Cupids Carnival is perfect for all ages. We have rides for the thrill seekers and ones for the people who are more on the calm side. Not only is the atmosphere and rides great, but the food trucks are one of a kind. There are multiple choices of food for you to choose from!


Angel Studio


Angel Studio is a magical museum for angel statues of all kinds, big and small or pleasing and frighting. Angel Studio is most popular for the "Broken Angel" as it has a sad meaning of abandonment seen from a childs eyes, the picture is placed below.


Corner Coffee


Welcome to corner Coffee, a place where busy people can drop buy and pick up some food and coffee on the way to work or where ever they need to be. On the menu, they have the popular national coffee. Doyalia latte, made from the sap of the Doyalia tree found in local forestry, and Angelina pudding, flavoured with the spices of the forest.


The Library


Even though a library doesn't have any awesome rides or music, it is still pretty fun to explore. The libraries here in Capyparlasen are massive and have more books then you could ever imagine, The plants that usually grow inside the libarry give the buildings a welcoming look and a nice natural aroma.

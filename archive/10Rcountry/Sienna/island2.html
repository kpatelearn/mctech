KAVA

From Hokuma, it takes just a 15 minute boat journey to Kava's stunning palm-lined beaches and sparkling bordering reefs.

The area is the ideal playground for water babies, with blooming reefs, huge waves, and a serene lagoon right outside their door. Take part in thrilling water activities like Surfing world-class waves, or ride exhilarating jet skis across the lagoon to get your heart pumping. Wind down with a unique stay at the Sierra Bungalow Resort to experience paradise.

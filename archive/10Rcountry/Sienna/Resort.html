PALMS RESORT



Located on the Serene Isles' main Island Hokuma, a tropical haven that visitors are sure to adore, is where the Palms Resort is situated.

A private patio or balcony with a garden or ocean view, a 32" LCD TV with premium channels, a soothing rainforest shower head in the bathroom, a coffee maker, slippers, bathrobes, an iron and an ironing board, free Wi-Fi, designer amenities, and a refrigerator are all included in each of their generous guest rooms.

The resort is perfect for families with the amazing kids programmes, kids clubs and activities that are offered. Visitors to the resort can relax by the resort's dazzling pool, splash around at the resorts own waterpark, play a game of family golf or workout at the 24-hour gym. The resort has its three own resturants that offer fine cuisine.

Facilities


Palms Resort has 3 resturants that offer exceptional cuisine. The resort has its very own waterpark with a todler splash play area and life guard supervision. The resort has just opened a newly renovated lobby and dining area with a modern architectural design. A 24-hour gym provides equipment and fitness programmes for all ages. Leave your kids at the kids club where your kids are looked after and have acess to an indoor playground and toys. Step Outside the resort and take a dip in the huge pool surrounded with beach cabanas and a bar with the beach just a few steps away. Play a game golf with the family while admiring the beach view.

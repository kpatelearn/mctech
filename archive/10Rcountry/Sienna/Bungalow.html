SIERRA BUNGALOW RESORT



Sierra Resort is a blissful and luxurious over water bungalow resort offering unmatched views and serenity on a secluded island called Kava. Visitors enjoy the largest private stretch of fine white sand beaches, which are tucked between the captivating lagoon.

Accommodations range from the roomy Lagoon View Suites, which are great for families, to the breathtaking overwater villas. With numerous restaurants and bars, there are many dining options that will satisfy your appetite. There are many exciting activities available, ranging from trekking to swimming with sharks. On the hilltop, unwind by the stunning infinity pool, which offers tranquilly and breath-taking views.

Facilities


Enjoy exceptional cuisine in stylish settings at the resort's many restaurants. From the open-air restaurant's comfortable banquet and veranda seating, enjoy cuisine from around the world while taking in the spectacular views of the lagoon. For Breakfast, go wild at the Buffet, which is open everyday (6am-10am). state-of-the-art cardio and weight training equipment are available at the 24-hour fitness centre. The resort offers a 1.5-mile beautiful running track in addition to fitness courses and personal training. The resort's shimmering infinity pool, complete with cabanas and a swim-up bar, is rated among the best 15 swimming pools in the world and is one of the resorts key features. Visitors can catch their breath at the Pool Bar, relax in the water, and enjoy mouthwatering appetisers and light meals while drinking cocktails.

Overwater Pool Bungalow


1 1

The Overwater Pool bungalow, which offers ultimate luxury, provides all you need for a comfortable and restful holiday! With the windows entirely retractable, you can extend the living space outside. Head to the roomy terrace to find the private infinity pool, a floating hammock over the water, and sun loungers. Take a swim or go snorkelling right from your balcony thanks to the direct access to the ocean. Take in the views of the sky and the breathtaking sunset before sleeping in the cosy king bed. A separate dining area, a roomy bathroom with a tub, a minibar, tea and coffeemaking equipment, an HDTV, and a Bluetooth sound system are also included in the villa's facilities.

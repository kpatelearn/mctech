A day in the life of a Kiddie!

8:30 am, is the latest time a Kiddie usually wakes up and starts their day. Kiddies always believe that the early bird gets the worm or as they say it the early kiddie gets the pikelets! A Kiddie will then wash their face and eat breakfast which is often fairy bread french toast and will be served by their loyal dog-chef. While eating their breakfast Kiddies watch the daily episode of Seasme street-The soap opera and sip their fluffies. (Fluffies are leftover frothed milk from coffee!). Next, a Kiddie will get ready for the day and by 9:10 am the cocomelon zumba class will begin. Kiddies will meet with their friends in their homes and complete the cocomelon zumba class until 9:30am. Free time continues, Kiddies typically play in the ballpit, go to the waterpark, catch up with friends and so much more. 6:00 PM all Kiddies are either home or at a restuarant ( Often Mcdonalds) for dinner. 9:00 PM, majority of Kiddies are home OR PARTYING. Parties are a huge hit in Kidtropolis, particularly on fridays, Kiddies will finish their duties at 2:00 PM and prepare for the friday parties. Bedtime is up to 12:00am and thats a day in the life of a Kiddie!

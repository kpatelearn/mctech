History

The Little Eatery was first created by Margret George, in 1978, who was at the time 23. She was born on the 4th of December and every since she was a kid she had a love for cooking and making people smile! When Margret was 22 she moved in with her single aunty who was feeling quite lonely. Together they made many dishes and created new recipes. Eventually, they decided to listen to all their friends and family and opened a restaurant in their house.

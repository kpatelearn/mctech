Accomodation and rooms

The hotel rooms and suites at Affluence offer the perfect blend of beauty and elegance.
Our rooms will elevate your stay, whether you're traveling for business, celebrating a honeymoon,
or just looking for a remarkable getaway.
With views of the 10s neighbourhood or the iconic Terrorworld theme park
right from your suite, your stay will be nothing short of unforgettable.

Lumières chaudes suite

Enjoy your stay with us and relax in the Lumières chaudes suite! Perfect for couples
Looking for a getaway and to spend some time together. Warm settings and lots of candles
make for a indescribable setting. With amazing city views your stay here will certainly
be satisfactory.


Divine suite

If your travelling with your family and are looking for a big room to accomodate you all,
then our Divine suite is for you! With a king size double bed, two sets of bunk beds
and two bathrooms, this suite will certainly be large enough for familys of up to six.


Sweet Suite

Our "Sweet Suite" is designed to be a one person suite. With enough room for one person,
you can enjoy a holiday getaway to yourself and not have to worry about getting in
others ways. Or if your staying for a quick business trip, have a appropriate size room,
for your stay.


Presidential suite

Our presidential suite is the best the 10S neighbourhood can offer.
With many celebrities having stayed in the room over the years such as Miss Papa,
Miss Lisboa and many more. If you chose to stay with us here you will have an experince
Of a lifetime.

Booked Groups and Complex Capacity

If a booked group arrives after we have reached Max Capacity they too will have to wait for other customers to leave before they enter. Unfortunately a booking will not guarantee you entry if we reach Maximum Capacity. We recommend all groups to arrive at 10am to try and avoid this issue.

Complex Capacity

There will be days during Summer where we will reach Maximum Capacity of visitors. At this stage we will ask any new customers to wait outside until other customers leave. Our Capacity limit is for your safety.

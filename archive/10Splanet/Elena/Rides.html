Ride out the storm on an awesome new coaster that the whole family will enjoy! Hold on tight as you face a 20 metre wave on dry land. Can you brave an extraordinary fire while drenched in water? Be prepared to ride the perfect storm, without a single cloud!

Whilst all guests must meet the height requirements to ride, some guests may be unable to experience this attraction due to the safety restraints and ride vehicle specifications.
• Attraction is not suitable for guests with back, neck or similar physical conditions
• Attraction is not suitable for guests with heart disorders, pacemaker or high blood pressure
• Attraction is not suitable for expectant mothers
• Attractions are not suitable for guests that have had recent surgery
• Attraction is not recommended for those susceptible to motion sickness
• Attraction is not recommended for guests susceptible to seizures
• Attraction is not recommended for guests with epilepsy
• Attraction is not suitable for guests with pre-existing limb injuries
• Attraction is not suitable for guests with any other physical limitations that could be aggravated by sudden fierce physical movements
• Guests using a wheelchair must transfer to ride vehicle’s seating at platform

For safety purposes some restrictions apply to guests with pre-existing medical conditions, an amputation, Amelia or prosthetic limbs. Please contact our Guest Services office on (07) 9115 546 for further information.

Access for guests using wheelchairs is via the main ride entrance. Guest wishing to ride this attraction must be able to:
• Follow the instructions of the ride attendant at all times
• Transfer into the boat independently or with the assistance of a carer
• Independently maintain an upright position whilst seated
• Remove all prosthesis unless unable to be removed
• Continually grasp the restraint with at least one fully functioning natural arm whilst bracing with two natural lower limbs

More Info

Ride out the storm on an awesome new coaster that the whole family will enjoy! Hold on tight as you face a 20 metre wave on dry land. Can you brave an extraordinary fire while drenched in water? Be prepared to ride the perfect storm, without a single cloud!

Health and Safety requirements:

-Transfer to and from the ride independently or with the assistance of a companion
-Independently maintain yourself in an upright position while seated
-Have full upper body movement and coordination
-Swim strongly for when you suddenly enter the 1m splash down pool at the end of the ride
-Exit the ride in a timely manner
Upper and lower limb prothesis must be removed, guest must still meet all rider requirements
-Guests with hard casts or hard supports will not be able to ride this attraction
-Suitable for a single rider only, tandem riding is not permitted
-Individual maximum weight is 135kg

THE DRIVE IN AND BUBBLE IN EXPERIENCE

The Ashwood Haven Community are excited to bring back Drive In Theatres to everyone who loves a good Movie! The Conduire au theatre is open 9am-5pm on Weekdays but we are excited to bring back Late Night Movies on Fri, Sat and Sundays!
Don't have a car? Book a Bubble In!

WHATS A BUBBLE IN?

Come and Enjoy the Experience in a Bubble-In-Dome atmosphere!
In Dome Dining costs $30.00 (including movie ticket as well as the Bubble In Dome)

The Bubble In allows those who do not have a car to relax and enjoy in a little dome instead of the boot of your car. The Dome can also be a place to dine in with your friends and family while you watch the Movie!

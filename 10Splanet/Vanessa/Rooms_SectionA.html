Luxury Suites & Residences (Section A)


Suite

These rooms are layed out different because they have the bedroom and the bathroom on the right hand side.
The kitchen on the left and the living room in front.
These rooms have a lot more space than normal residences.




Type 1

These rooms are layed out normally with a kitchen on the right hand side. The living room right in front.
And a hallway on the left leading to a bathroom, bedroom, and laundry room.




Type 2

These rooms are different from the other room, they are also smaller than the regular residences.
On the right is the kitchen.
At the end of the room there are some double doors which lead into the bedroom.
Just before the double doors there are two doors. One is to the bathroom and the other is the laundry room.

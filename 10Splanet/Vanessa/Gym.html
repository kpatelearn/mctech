Gym & Sauna


Gym & Yoga rooms

At the main part of the building when you enter there is two staircases at back.
The one on the left leads to the Gym, and the one on the right leads to the Restaurant.

There are 12 treadmills, 6 weight lifts, and 6 punching bags.
Infront of the treadmill there are 3 tvs, so you dont get bored.

There is a yoga room with a radio on the right hand side of the room.




Lockers & Shower

At the back to of gym there is a double door which leads to some lockers.
And on the right hand side of the locker room there is a shower.

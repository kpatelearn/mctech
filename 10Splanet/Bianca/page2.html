INFORMATION




Terror World was created to help everyone have fun and memorable experience.
Established in 2022, Terror World’s friendly staff and colourful environment
let’s our visitors feel welcome and enjoy their time.
Terror World is located in the 10s Neighbourhood,
many visitors like to stay at hotels such as Affluence and The View Hotel.
These hotels are right next door and an easy 5-10 minute walk away from the park.
Terror World is a collection of adventures, rides, and fair games.
Terror can be enjoyed by adults, teenagers and children.




With intense but safe rides, you are sure to enjoy every minute you spend at Terror World.
Some amazing prizes can be won from our insanely entertaining fair games and you can enjoy underwater dining but with a twist. A ride will go through the restaurant.
Terror world helps you escape reality and lets you have fun all day.
Terror World is the place to be, and you sure are going to be able to enjoy yourself with friends and family.

L'hôtel aux Papillons

A five star hotel sits on the water's edge, with gorgeous views of the ocean and the city. This hotel has been running for 20 years, and has hosted royal families, world leaders, and many celebrities. With 400 rooms, pools, tennis courts, gyms, and a spa, this hotel is also home to one of the country's best restaurants with award winning chefs.

S P A & G Y M

The jewel in the crown of this hotel is its stunning rooftop Sky Spa. A sanctuary above the hustle and bustle of Maddiegascar, it’s a place for guests to unwind and recharge after a day on the slopes. Go for a swim in the heated outdoor infinity pool surrounded by mountain peaks for an unforgettable experience. Back inside, there are two saunas: a bio sauna and a dry sauna, both offering views over the mountains and sea. Or simply sit back and unwind in the relaxation area, where you can enjoy panoramic views of the mountains. There is also an outdoor relaxation area where you can soak up the sun. Rediscover adventure in the unique natural movement gym. Classes and state-of-the-art personal training studios offer a mix of Yoga, Pilates, cardio and strength training all day. Float away in the tranquil spa, with an expansive pool, sauna, hammam, hydropool and experience shower. Reduce stress, improve strength and agility naturally, relax and re-awaken your senses.

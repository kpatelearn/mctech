Activities

In upside down town there is lots fun things to do. Our most popular place for tourists to visit is the bird swimming pool.

I know the name bird swimming pool doesnt sound too apitising to most people, it is very clean and the birds are very friendly. Its the perfect idea to cool off after a hot day. Make sure to bring your goggles.

Another popular activity in upside down town is the moon racetrack. To get here you take a quick rocket trip to the moon.

A very underated activity to do is going for a fly. The fish are very well trained at flying and will care for you. Please dont take kids under the age of 7.

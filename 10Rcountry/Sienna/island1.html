HOKUMA

Hokuma is Serene Isles' main island that is home to the best beaches in the world. The lengthy shoreline beaches have crystal clear water, great surf, fine white sand, and palm-lines. Homuka also offers great family accommodation right near the beach at Palms Resort.

More adventures await you on land along hiking trails that are swallowed up by dense rainforest and re-emerge at stunning view points. The Upper Hokuma River snakes around this unique tropical wilderness that is hidden from view and is fed by numerous waterfalls, shielded by treacherous rapids, and covered under a thick canopy of rainforest. Go above the trails and soar above the rainforest on a speedy zip line taking in the breathtaking view of Hokuma.

Hokuma's top rated beaches, lush rainforest, clear skies, and plenty of sunshine can make you happy for days, whether you're looking for an exhilarating outdoor experience or eager for some relaxed luxury.

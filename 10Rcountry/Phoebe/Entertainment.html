Cupids Carnival


Known for its fun rides and romantic atmosphere, Cupids Carnival is perfect for all ages. We have rides for the thrill seekers and ones for the people who are more on the calm side. Not only is the atmosphere and rides great, but the food trucks are one of a kind. There are multiple choices of food for you to choose from!


Angel Studio


Angel Studio is a magical museum for angel statues of all kinds, big and small or pleasing and frighting. Angel Studio is most popular for the "Broken Angel" as it has a sad meaning of abandonment seen from a childs eyes, the picture is placed below.

ABOUT THIS CITY

PINKTOWN is a city all about pink. Most foods are pink, most buildings are pink, the sand at our main tourist beach is pink! This city is where actor BARBIE was born, raised and where 'Barbie, LIFE IN A DREAM HOUSE' was filmed. Our city had gotten reconition from BARBIE, and now it is famous for being pink and it's gorgtastic tourist sights + activities.

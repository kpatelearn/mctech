Dusty Bay Carnival

Dusty Bay carnival is located right beside Dusty Bay. The entry fee for the day is $20. Some of the carnival activities include:



Ferris wheel

The only solar powered ferris wheel is located right here in Sunny Sun City!! The ferris wheel offers a beautiful view of dusty bay and the coastline. It takes around 15 minutes to get around once. Many people who come here highly recomend going on the feris at least once, if not more.

"was the most beautiful veiw. Went on right before sunset and had the privledge to see the coastline with the sunset in the background. 10/10 would recomend"
- asor rebam

Water Gun Race

Players shoot their water towards the target. As the water hits the target, a stuffed animal or other icon, races to top towards the finish. The first player to reach the finish and ring bell wins a prize. Many prizes are offered to those who win!!

"Super fun game to play with the family!! Managed to win a prize or two aswell!"
- ranga powre

Roll-a-Ball

Guests must roll their ball to one of the three speed zones. When their ball lands into one of the holes, their character on the playfield moves closer to the finish line. The higher the zone, the further the character moves. The first to reach the finish line wins! There are many stuffed animals up for grab!

"wish I had won this game, there were so many prizes i wanted!! It was super fun!"
- alle kirck

Carousel

Perfect ride for family and children! You hop on the platform the will start rotating when the ride starts. When you get on, you can sit on the horses or whatever else is there. Parents are allowed to stand next to their kids aswell.

"took my daughter on this and she absolutly loved it!! when we got home she wouldnt stop talking about how fun it was! Definitly reccomend going if you have kids"
- branks sun












































Lizert Lizerty

Lizert Lizerty carnival is the highest rated carnival in all of tenarvia!! some attractions include :



Basketball Shoots

Awesome game for competitive friends!! You have 1 minute to get as many shots as you can into the basketball hoop. You can play against your friends to see who can get the most shots! this game has always been popular amoungst people. Shoot enough goals and have a chance to win tickets

"so much fun playing against my friends!""
-leyod yalc

Dunking game

Do you want a chance to dunk your friends in water? Well.. this is the game for you!! One person will go and it on the chair while the others try to hit a bullseye. If the bullseye is hit, the chair will fall dunking the person in the water. Bring your friends along to see if you can dunk them!

"i got to dunk my friend lol"
- eiddam kralc

Tub Toss

The goal of the game is to throw the ball in the bucket without it falling out. It may seem like an easy goal, but the slope of the bucket makes it more complicated for you. If you manage to get 3 balls in, you win a prize!!

"much more difficult than i thought. super fun challenge tho!""
- jen jenny

Bottle stand

Players attempt to stand the bottle with the rod and string! The winner is determined by if the participant can successfully stand the bottle. this game is a fun challenge that anyone can enjoy. If you manage to make it stand, you will win a prize

"wowowowowow! was like super duper fun"
- danny phantom

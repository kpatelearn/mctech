Beaches

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

If there is one thing that Sunny Sun City is known for, it's our beaches! All of our beaches have a zero rubbish policy which helps maintain their beautiful scenery!

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


El Bay

El bay is a very relaxing and peaceful beach. It has lots of walks that are available to go on while at the beach. It has a beautiful sunrise view and is the perfect temperature to go swimming. El bay is also the beach connected to the resort. See Accommodation for more information

Bunka Beach

The perfect beach for families!! It is a perfect swimming beach for the kids. There is little to no surf, and it is safe for paddling. T here is a playground suitable for kids on a large grass area. The grassy area has shade for picnics and barbeques. Be sure to check it out!!



Dusty bay!

Dusty bay is the perfect beach to go to for a fun day out! Not only does it have a good surf, there is also a Carnival you can go to right next to it!! The water is always a nice temperature at dusty bay - not too warm, but also not too hot! There are also many food trucks around so no need to bring a packed lunch!

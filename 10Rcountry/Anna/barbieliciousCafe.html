Barbielicious Cafe

The best cafe im all of tenarvia!! From the silky coffee, to the
fresh strawberries and creme, you will always find something you love in here!
It is located right next to the beach so it is the perfect place to pick up a coffee,
or even stay in for lunch while sitting in the outside seating and looking at the veiw. Even when your sitting inside you can admire the
beautifuly decorated room. It really does feel like heaven in there!

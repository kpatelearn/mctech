Rosa's Hotdogs

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The Hotdog truck that is always there when you need it.

~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~


Where to find?

Going from one singular van and worker, to many vans spread out all across Sunny Sun City. Rosa's hotdog truck has been a favourite amoungst the locals. It all started off with one person with a dream. Rosa started up her buisness and sat by Dusty Bay all day serving her customers. Soon, word spread of her delicous hotdogs and people came travilling just to get one. Due to the overwelming support, Rosa was able to hire more workers and vans to help accomidate the demand. However this success & fame has not stopped her from doing what she loves. You can still find her inher original truck down by dusty bay serving customers. However Dusty bay is not the only place you can buy from her. There are lots of trucks spread out by the other beaches, Carnivals and anywhere you can go.

Nature On Planet Her

In most of Planet Her you will see a lot of beautiful aspects of nature such as magic seaweed,
fire spouts and dewdrop sun flowers.

MAGIC SEAWEED:

This type of seaweed feel many of the sea creatures on Planet Her, in the night, when its dark
outside you can see these seaweed plants from far away because of its emmence green glow. At night
the ocean floors look amazing, the full sea is lit up and you can see lots of sea animals swimming
around late at night.

FIRE SPOUTS:

Fire spouts are little pretty flames that sit on top of water, you can only see it during the day
because the sun creates them. These flames are a bright blue color with a darker blue rim around it.

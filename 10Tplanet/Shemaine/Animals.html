Animals On Planet Her

Planet Her has many differnt living things, apart from the civilisation, this planet has, mythic
fire dragons that are immune to cool surface waters and fire bred phoenixes made from ashes of
past relatives.

Mythic fire dragons:

These creatures eat magic seaweed, they grow at the bottom of the ocean, and the dragons find
them since they have a sense that can see them from miles away since the magic seaweed glows
a bright green colour which helps the dragons find their way to their food

Fire bred phoenixes:

The phoenixes on Planet Her, they are a orange color and are formed from the ashes of our past
family as a reminder that they are always watching. They usually are found sitting on the high
tree tops or soaring across the water

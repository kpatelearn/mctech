Habitats On Planet Her

On Planet Her people live in spaceships, your rank decides your house, for example if you get a good
rank you get a good house or if you get a bad rank you get a bad house. These houses come in all different
shapes and sizes and can be found all over the placel, way up in the mountains or down flat on the ground.
If you get a house and you're not happy with it you must complete a variety of tasks during the year and
hope that your zodiac group gets chosen the following year.

The houses are all differnt colors but some of the most rare ones are:
- Teal
- Yellow
- Pink
- White
- Orange

If you end up getting a high ranked house then it could be:
- Black
- Holographic
- White
- Pink

Usually the high ranked houses also have a lot of windows to show how high it is ranked

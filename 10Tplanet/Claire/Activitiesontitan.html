ACTIVITIES

Cool Zone

Cool Zone is a themepark created 99 years ago on this planet. The rides are amazing and this is a very popular place where people hang out. These rides include, the flying canyon, the drop of death and the cranky teacups.

Splash Zone

Splash Zone is a highly rated waterpark in Titan. It has amazing slides and even a childrens area (under 10), where children can also have fun but be safe. For people older than 10, there are many different slides including the, drop of death, the aqua loop and the Tsunami Surge.


Adeventure Zone

Adventure Zone is a two part place, it has a bowling alley and a ice skating part. All ages are welcome!

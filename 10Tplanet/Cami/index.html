Guide to the Planet of Tardonia

What is your name?





This purple planet is located in the 10T Universe, it is round, surrounded by a blue sky and stars. The planet is completely purple in color, including most things on its surface and is home to a wide variety of extra-terrestrial life, including species like butterflies, aliens and ants with big wings. Not so far away is a bright yellow moon that shines its light onto Tardonia. You can find mountains, rivers, trees and windy weather on Tardonia.

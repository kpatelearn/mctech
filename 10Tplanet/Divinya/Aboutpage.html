My Planet was inspired by the book series 'The Medoran Chronicles' by Lynette Noni. The series contains 5 Books - Akarnae, Raelia, Draekora, Graevale and Vardesia - There is also an extra book called 'We three heroes'. The author has also written a Medoran Chronicles short story in 2 parts titled 'A VERY MEDORAN KALDORAS'.

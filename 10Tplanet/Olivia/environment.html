The Environment


Steorra is a beautifu planet, filled with many unique plants and animals. ALthough most of the planet is purple ocean, the small bits of land are beautiful, with towering blue trees, pink leaves and flowers with fairy-dust like particles around them. The almost overgrown forests are easy to get lost in - with all the vines and bushes everywhere. The sky is just as magical - purple with a bit of yellow during day, and fully yellow clouds during night.

The climate is different from Earth's own - it is often always raining, except on two or three days of their year.

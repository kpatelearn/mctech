RADIOACTIVE BIRDS
These birds of all species that have somehow found their way
to Waderham. In the warmer time of the year, they all fly down
to the darkside because they like to stay cool. While down there,
they drink from the radioactive lake which gives them their green
glow and gives them sustanance for the rest of the year.

Things to do in Acuatica

The most popular activity on Acuatica is rock jumping.
There are so many rocks everywhere and you can do it for free so everyone really enjoys it.

If you have a phone, you may like to try communicate with creatures from other planets.
The nearest and easiest planets to reach are Evvit and Eslie.

The waterpark is the funnest thing to do on Acuatica.
It's quite expensive but it's totally worth it.

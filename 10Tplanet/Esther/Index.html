Millions of years ago, Acuatica was just like Earth. There were citys and blue skies. But slowly the water level started rising and the sky started turning orange.
Now, the whole plant is underwater and the sky gives the illuison that its a pretty sunset, but it's just the smoke in the sky.
The planet was renamed from Tierra, which is land in Spanish, to Acuatica, which is water in Spanish.

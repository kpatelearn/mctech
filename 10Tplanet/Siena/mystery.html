Learn about Cebronix

The planet Cebronix was created 1 Quintillion years ago.
It came to be when 2 Gods, Shemaini and Willini got into
a big argument and used their powers against eachother.
Shemaini's power is having mystical matter appear whenever
and Willini's being she can spawn anything his mind thinks
of. Willini thought of Nuclear matter and when it met
with Shemaini's mystical matter the planet CEBRONIX formed.

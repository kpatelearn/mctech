All about Nidavellir

Nidavellir has been alive for more than 500000 years. Through out the years the planet has grown more in population and buildings. Nobody knows who created the planet or if it created itself but what we do know is the history of the planet and anything else you need to know.

These places are two different places that has existed for 30000 years called "Natures Valley" and "Spirit arena" which right outside the planet. Natures Valley is quite far away from the main city so you must travel by the flying train. These places are Historic in Nidavellir that you must visit while you are here, but be careful with the "Golbers" which are creatures that live in the planet on the right.

the environment ☀

The environment on Sandstorm is very dangerous for those inexperienced in exploration. There are seas of quicksand all around the planet that u need a trained eye to avoid. The temperature ranges from around 30-50c this gives non-natives a hard time due to them not being used to this heat. There are 3 lakes scatted among the planet where they get most of their water from. They do not need water to survive it is only used to wash their gold. Their gravity levels are about 20% more than earth making it easier to fly with their wings. Many explorers have tried to create an accurate map but all have failed due to its size and large terrain.

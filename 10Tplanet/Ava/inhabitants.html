Mr Paterson & Stephan

Mr Paterson is the Profit, he created Veenbessen with his bear hands. He loves the color pink, he can do anything without incorporating it in everything. Including his beautiful sunglasses that him and his best friend, the boar, Stephan wear.

Him and Stephan are inseparable, Mr Paterson saved Stephan from a ginormous 7 Legged Ellefant and in order to re-pay him Stephan built his Pear home with him. They are as close as close and their friendship is thriving.

Sky Whale

This bizarre creature turns out to be a large flying mammal It's about the size of a school bus, has four strong side fins, a huge dorsal fin and a large and a long powerful tail.

They live in the sky, resting in the clouds. They're omnivors, sometimes flying down and eating large clumps of beans, as well as the flying Salamander, native to this planet

Stingrays

Stingrays have broad fins that run the full length of their bodies, giving them a flat, roundish shape.

To fly, some stingrays move their whole bodies in a wavy motion that propels them through the sky. Other species flap their fins like bird wings and fly through the sky.

Auodad

The Aoudad is a goat-like creature with curious wings sprouted from their backs, the usually white feathery wings keep them in the air and help get them away from predators.

They have large teeth that are specifically made for chewing and grinding all of the beans they consume

Electric Pigeons

Pigeons are aggressive and plump, All pigeons strut about with a characteristic bobbing of the head. Because of their long wings and powerful flight muscles, they are strong, swift fliers.

Their electricity relates to their mating world. The larger and louder their electricity cloud is the more a female pigeon will want them

Gaint Jerboa

The jerboa is a large, hopping rodent that lives in the mountains of Pear Bay and Tooka Lane. Jerboas are incredibly cute animals and have very long ears, tails, and hind feet that give them an almost cartoonish appearance. They also have specially adapted legs that allow them to move about by jumping like a kangaroo.

7 Legged Ellefant

They have large, thin ears that are used to help cool them down, and have long, powerful trunks. Their trunks can grow to be about six feet long, and can sense the size, shape, and temperature of an object.

The most unique feature of these animals is that they have adapted to their habitats by growing 3 extra legs in order to migrate across the Parmesan deserts

Wilde Boar

As the most advanced species on the planet, Wild Boars sit at the top of the food chain, they catch some of their food from the Cranberry Rivers, most the food being Prarie Dogs and Moose Hooves.

They can speak fluent English and have extremely posh accents. Extraordinarily they have developed their own kind of music, very similar to Earth’s classical. They use hollowed-out beans as flute-like instruments and tap garlic bread as drums.

Wailing Tooka

The song of the Wailing Tooka is a loud, complex series of sounds repeated over and over. It occurs primarily (although not exclusively) during the breeding season, is sung only by males, and its composition changes as it is being sung with all singers in a population singing the same version at any one time.

With their charismatic nature, they enjoy performing these songs (or ‘wails’ as scientists call them) too large groups of Lowland Woc and Wild Boar.

Cranbry Moose

These species of Meese live in the Bays and Estreés of Veenbessen, they slowly developed large flippers instead of hooves and made their antler a lot smaller so they can easily get away from Wild Boar.

Cranberry Moose have large communities under the Cranberry Juice and have been seen to make friends with Parie Dogs

King Julian

King Julian is not actually native to Veenbessen, from talking to the Boars scientist learned that he crashed onto Veenbessen on a meteor and decided to stay there.

He brought in all the large plants that the natives decide to reside in. some of the plants he imported are; Mangos, Sweeties and Pears.

Lowland Woc

Lowland Woc are friendly countryside cattle that mainly belong to the Bean Feilds. It is a hardy breed with long horns and a shaggy coat that can withstand high winds, heavy juicefalls, and cheesey climatic conditions.

Lowlands are herbivores that depend entirely on Beans, Lemon Leaves, and Garlic Breadfor their survival.

Flying Salamander

This amazing, but often seen creature is a type of amphibian. It's about the size of a newt, has two legs and two arms and a long, strong tail. They have a thick, but delicate skin covered in a thin layer of mucous, which is usually either brown, dark bronze, orange or silver or a combination of these colors.

They live in marshy areas and are relatively rare. They're carnivores and their relatively small mouths, their teeth and rough tongue are ideal for eating creatures

Cranbery Prairie Dog

The Prairie Dog are a small Aquatic mammal that live in the shellows of the Cranberry rivers, they eat Beans off the sea floor. They have large front teeth in order to get the beans of the stalks and small hairs cover their body.

They have a dolphin like personality and enjoy leeping out of the water, they chatter amongst eachother with small squeals.

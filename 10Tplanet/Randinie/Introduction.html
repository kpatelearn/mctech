Welcome to Rotopia. A place where there are no limits with imagination. Rotopia is a safe mindspace where it relies on your imagination, your brain, your personality and your wants. Rotopia is a planet that is my home. There are certain boundaries to keep the beautiness of certain living things alive, in the centre there is a city where there are festivities and gatherings but other than that there are also endless designs of houses. On this website I will be showing the environment, population, some morals and photos. My planet as you know is named Rotopia. I got this name from merging Rosanna/Randinie with Utopia. Hence the Ro in Rotopia. As you can see Rotopia is a purple hemisphere because I like the colour purple. I read somewhere that purple can stand for wisdom, bravery and spirituality. Also can express sadness and frustration.

Password Rules


Passwords must be at least 12 characters long.

Use a combination of numbers, lowercase and uppercase letters, and symbols

Avoid easy to guess words or numbers, like your pets name or school name, or birth date etc.

Each password you use should be totally different from all other passwords you use.

To help you rememebr all your different passwords, you will need a good, secure, reliable password manager.
